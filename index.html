More Than a Destination

India isn't just a place on the map. It's a feeling you carry with you long after you've left: the scent of jasmine in the morning air, temple bells echoing through narrow lanes, a stranger who won't let you go without one more cup of chai. From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas to the backwaters of Kerala, every corner has its own story, its own language, its own flavour.

Visit India and you won't leave the way you came. You'll arrive as a traveller and leave shaped by conversations you didn't expect, moments you couldn't have planned, and a culture that somehow feels both ancient and very much alive. Through AIESEC's exchange programs, you don't just see India, you live in it: working alongside local communities, taking on real projects, and figuring out a version of yourself you didn't know was there.

“
India isn't just somewhere you visit. It's somewhere that stays with you long after you've gone home. I've watched exchange participants land confused on day one and leave a few weeks later talking about India like it's a second home, because that's what tends to happen here. You'll work on something that actually matters, pick up a phrase or two of a language you never planned to learn, and probably get invited to a stranger's wedding at some point. That's just how it goes. So come find out for yourself why we call it India is a Feeling, because once you've felt it, you'll know exactly what we mean.
Rasul Himmath Member Committee President, AIESEC India 2026, 2027

Our Exchange Programs

Three ways to experience India, each one built to push you, teach you something new, and change how you see things.

Global Volunteer

A 6–8 week volunteering program for ages 18–30. You'll work on projects tied to the UN Sustainable Development Goals and get a real taste of Indian culture along the way.

  • Cross-cultural leadership development
  • Community impact projects
  • Full cultural immersion
Learn More

Global Talent

A professional internship that gives your career a real boost in the global market, while you pick up new skills and contribute to some of India's fastest-growing industries.

  • Professional skill development
  • Industry exposure in India
  • Global career network
Learn More

Global Teacher

Teach in Indian schools and communities, helping bridge real gaps in education while picking up just as much from your students as they do from you.

  • Classroom teaching experience
  • Culturally responsive learning
  • Educational impact
