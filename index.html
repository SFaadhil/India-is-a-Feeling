More Than a Destination

India isn't just a place on the map — it's a feeling you carry with you long after you've left. It's the scent of jasmine in the morning air, the sound of temple bells echoing through narrow lanes, the warmth of a stranger who insists you stay for chai. From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas to the sun-soaked backwaters of Kerala, every corner tells a different story, speaks a different language, and tastes like nothing else on earth.

To visit India is to be transformed. You arrive as a traveller and leave as something more — shaped by conversations you didn't expect, moments you couldn't plan, and a culture so ancient and so alive that it rewires the way you see the world. Through AIESEC's exchange programs, you won't just see India — you'll live it. You'll work alongside local communities, contribute to something real, and discover a version of yourself you never knew existed.

Our Exchange Programs

Three pathways to experience India — each designed to challenge, inspire, and transform.

Global Volunteer

A 6–8 week volunteering program for ages 18–30. Work on projects aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals while immersing yourself in Indian culture.
