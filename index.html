More Than a Destination

India isn't just a place on the map — it's a feeling you carry with you long after you've left. From the snow-capped Himalayas to the tropical coasts of Kerala, every corner offers a new story, a new flavour, a new perspective.

Through AIESEC's exchange programs, you'll go beyond tourism. You'll live with local communities, work on impactful projects, and discover a version of yourself you never knew existed.

Our Exchange Programs

Three pathways to experience India — each designed to challenge, inspire, and transform.

Global Volunteer

A 6–8 week volunteering program for ages 18–30. Work on projects aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals while immersing yourself in Indian culture.

  • Cross-cultural leadership development
  • Community impact projects
  • Full cultural immersion
Learn More

Global Talent

A professional internship experience that accelerates your career in a global market. Gain real-world skills while contributing to India's dynamic industries.

  • Professional skill development
  • Industry exposure in India
  • Global career network
Learn More

Global Teacher

Bridge educational gaps by teaching in Indian schools and communities. Develop culturally responsive pedagogy while making a lasting impact on young learners.

  • Classroom teaching experience
  • Culturally responsive learning
  • Educational impact
Learn More
