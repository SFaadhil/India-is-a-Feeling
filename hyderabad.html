Discover Hyderabad

Hyderabad is a city that feels like a warm embrace. Known as the City of Pearls, it seamlessly blends royal Mughal heritage with cutting-edge tech innovation. Late-night drives with chai, laughter that stretches past midnight, and biryani that tastes better in good company — that's the Hyderabad experience.

AIESEC in Hyderabad has been operating since 1985, making it one of India's most established local chapters and a top-performing committee. With over 35 years of hosting exchange participants, Hyderabad knows how to deliver Kiraak Experiences — unforgettable moments that stay with you for life.

City Highlights

What makes Hyderabad unforgettable.

Royal Mughlai Cuisine

Hyderabad's biryani is legendary worldwide. From Dum Biryani to haleem and double ka meetha, every meal is a royal affair.

Indo-Islamic Architecture

The iconic Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Qutb Shahi Tombs showcase centuries of Indo-Islamic architectural brilliance.

Bustling Streets

From the pearl markets of Laad Bazaar to Moazzam Jahi Market, Hyderabad's streets are alive with colour and commerce.

Green City

Awarded World Green City 2022, Hyderabad leads India's environmental sustainability movement.

Tech Hub of India

Home to HITEC City and major global tech companies, Hyderabad is India's innovation powerhouse.

City of Pearls

Historically the global centre of the pearl trade, Hyderabad's legacy of pearls and precious stones sparkles on.

Hussain Sagar Lake

The iconic heart-shaped lake stretching across the city centre, home to the Buddha statue and scenic waterfront evenings.

Ramoji Film City

The world's largest film studio complex — a sprawling entertainment universe and a fascinating glimpse into Indian cinema's creative magic.

Irani Chai Culture

Hyderabad's beloved Irani chai cafés are institutions — sip thick milky chai with Osmania biscuits and watch the city come alive at any hour.

Practical Information

Everything you need to know before arriving in Hyderabad.

Accommodation

Arranged through your host entity. Options include staff quarters, PGs, and hostels — prioritised for proximity to your workplace.

Cost of Living

Minimum ₹10,000 per month, varying based on your opportunity's included benefits. Hyderabad offers excellent value.

Transportation

Excellent connectivity via cabs (Ola/Uber), bikes (rental), metro rail, and local TSRTC buses. The metro is fast and affordable.

Visa

A student visa is required for exchange participants. Your local committee will guide you through the full application process.

Health & Safety

Hyderabad has world-class healthcare facilities. Your local committee provides comprehensive safety guidelines and 24/7 support.

Language

Telugu and Urdu are the local languages, with Hindi and English widely spoken. Hyderabad's cosmopolitan nature makes communication easy.

SIM Card & Internet

Jio and Airtel offer excellent 4G/5G coverage across Hyderabad. Pick up a prepaid SIM at the airport or any authorised store with just your passport.

Cultural Etiquette

Dress modestly at mosques, temples, and heritage sites. Remove footwear at religious entrances. A simple "Adaab" or "Namaste" goes a long way with locals.

Best Time to Visit

November to February offers pleasant weather. Summers (March–May) are hot at 35–40°C. Monsoon (June–September) brings greenery and cooler temperatures.

Emergency Contacts

Police: 100 · Ambulance: 108 · Fire: 101. Your local committee provides a 24/7 emergency contact number before you arrive in Hyderabad.

Food & Diet

Hyderabad caters to all diets — from legendary Dum Biryani to South Indian vegetarian. Inform your host about dietary needs and they'll ensure you're well fed.
