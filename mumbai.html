Discover Mumbai

Mumbai is India's maximum city — a vast, relentless, intoxicating metropolis that runs on ambition, coffee, and the collective dream of millions of people who came here to make something of themselves. The financial capital of India, the home of Bollywood, the city with the most billionaires outside of Beijing and New York — Mumbai is not a city you visit; it is a city that happens to you.

AIESEC in Mumbai places exchange participants at the beating heart of India's most dynamic city. From the colonial grandeur of Colaba to the startup energy of Lower Parel, from the chaos of Dharavi to the serenity of Bandra's sea face — Mumbai delivers on its promise of limitlessness in every direction you choose to look.

City Highlights

What makes Mumbai unforgettable.

Gateway of India

The iconic 1924 basalt arch on Mumbai's waterfront — the city's most recognisable landmark and the gateway through which millions have entered India's greatest city.

Dharavi & Community Spirit

Dharavi — one of Asia's largest urban communities — is a remarkable story of entrepreneurship, innovation, and human resilience in the heart of Mumbai's geography.

Bollywood & Film Culture

Mumbai is the home of Bollywood — India's extraordinary film industry that produces more films than any other in the world and shapes global popular culture.

Street Food Capital

From Vada Pav and Pav Bhaji to Mohammed Ali Road's Mughlai delicacies and the legendary Britannia & Co's berry pulao — Mumbai's street food is India's best.

Marine Drive

The Queen's Necklace — Marine Drive's sweeping art-deco seafront promenade is Mumbai's most beloved gathering spot for sunrise runs, evening walks, and monsoon storm watching.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus

The UNESCO World Heritage Victorian Gothic railway terminal — CST is not just a station, it is a cathedral of the city's colonial past and its pulsing, relentless present.

Financial & Startup Hub

From Dalal Street's stock exchange to the startup corridors of BKC and Lower Parel — Mumbai drives India's economy and remains the undisputed capital of Indian business.

Nightlife & Culture

Bandra's cafés and bars, Colaba's jazz clubs, Lower Parel's rooftop restaurants — Mumbai's after-dark culture is India's most vibrant, diverse, and truly 24-hour.

Practical Information

Everything you need to know before arriving in Mumbai.

Accommodation

Accommodation in proximity to your workplace is essential and will be arranged by your local committee. Options range from shared flats to PG accommodations across Mumbai's many neighbourhoods.

Cost of Living

Mumbai is India's most expensive city. Budget a minimum of ₹15,000–20,000 per month for transport, meals, and daily expenses. Accommodation costs are separate and arranged by your local committee.

Transportation

Mumbai's local train network is the city's lifeline — fast, cheap, and extraordinary. The Metro, BEST buses, and Ola/Uber cabs complete a comprehensive transport network across all of Greater Mumbai.

Visa

You'll need a valid Indian visa for your exchange. Your local committee will provide a detailed visa guide and assist with the application process.

Health & Safety

Mumbai has India's finest private hospitals — Lilavati, Kokilaben, Breach Candy, and Hinduja. Your local committee provides comprehensive health protocols and emergency support for all exchange participants.

Language

Marathi is the official language; Hindi and English are widely spoken across Mumbai's cosmopolitan population. The city's diversity means you'll hear a dozen languages on any single local train journey.

SIM Card & Internet

All major carriers — Jio, Airtel, Vi — offer excellent 4G/5G across Mumbai. SIMs are available at CSIA Airport and every mobile store with your passport. Data plans are affordable despite Mumbai's high cost of living.

Cultural Etiquette

Mumbai is India's most cosmopolitan city — dress codes are relaxed and the culture is open. At religious sites like Siddhivinayak Temple, dress modestly and observe queue etiquette. The city rewards energy, ambition, and genuine hustle.
