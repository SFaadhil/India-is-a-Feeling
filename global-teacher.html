The Classroom Is Where India Comes Alive

Global Teacher is AIESEC's teaching exchange — a program for educators and aspiring educators who believe learning can change lives. In India you'll teach English, STEM, and life skills in schools and community learning centres, working alongside local teachers who will teach you just as much as you teach them.

Few experiences shape you like standing in front of a classroom in another culture. You'll learn to communicate across languages, adapt your methods to new contexts, and watch students light up because you showed up. When you leave, the lessons stay — on both sides of the blackboard.
